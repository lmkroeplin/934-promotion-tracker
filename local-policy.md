## Scoring Structure

Three core value categories with different scoring scales:

### INTEGRITY (0, 1, 2, 3 scale)
1. Positive attitude around subordinates; exhibits commitment to unit & duty
2. Strong leadership & Intestinal Fortitude - can make unpopular decisions the troops will follow
3. Understands and carries out orders; can explain virtue of those orders, regardless
4. Has supervisor responsibilities and/or writes EPR's
5. 3-year attendance record: Absent 0-2 periods = 3pts, Absent 3-4 periods = 2pts, Absent 5-6 periods = 1pt
6. Participated in ARA, ORI, or Other Inspection in last three years
7. Currently performs an additional squadron duty
8. Outstanding military appearance and personal fitness

### SERVICE BEFORE SELF (0, 1, 2 scale)
1. Handles adversity and personal situations maturely (finances, health, home, travel, work, etc.)
2. First to arrive & last to leave attitude
3. 100% participation in last Bivouac
4. No delinquent or failed CDC's past 18 months (not voluntary courses)
5. 7 level in current Duty AFSC
6. Participated in last Silver Flag in the last three years
7. Participated in an AEF deployment in last three years
8. Initiates projects – exhibits and instills a sense of teamwork

### EXCELLENCE IN ALL WE DO (0, 1 scale unless noted)
1. Currently performs a shop additional duty
2. Attended NCO LDP
3. 2nd additional duty or Past additional duty within 3 yrs
4. Participated in one Formal Military function in last three years (Ball, Dining In/Out)
5. Completed NCO Academy (Course 6 or 9)
6. Completed SNCO Academy (Course 5, 8, 12 or 14)
7. Regularly uses vPC-GR, AFTR, ADLS, AF Portal
8. Technical competency / Expert in their field (0, 1, 2 scale)

### BONUS POINTS
- Interview Pts (by Commander request): 0, 1, 2, 3
- Participated in last UCI, worked on MICT checklist: 0, 2
- CCAF or College degree: 0, 1

### BEHAVIORAL ITEMS (Y/N - not scored but documented)
- Does the member always:
  a. Attend scheduled training
  b. Take notes to retain and pass on information
  c. Use the chain of command

### PROMOTION THRESHOLDS
| Rank | Minimum Points | Minimum for PEP |
|------|---------------|-----------------|
| SMSgt (E-8) | 38 | 44 |
| MSgt (E-7) | 32 | 38 |
| TSgt (E-6) | 26 | 32 |

- **Maximum Points:** 48
- Members in overtime training may not be promoted
- Commander's Note: Good Attitude, Performance and Participation are not occasional, convenient, or one time occurrences; they are continuous characteristics
